About

Sensory Percussion is creating a next-generation electronic drum that captures the true expressive nature of drumming. Used with any acoustic drum with a standard metal rim, it can capture the full acoustic range of the drum and allow for intricate and natural mappings to any sound desired, from samples to analog and software drum synthesizers. Effects follow your playing in real-time–no more knobs to deal with.

  Designed for the stage

Sensory Percussion was built for live performance. It has low latency and a user interface designed for the stage.

  Record drums without a big studio.

Recording drums is hard. You need thousands of dollars worth of microphones and recording equipment. And if you want a big sound, big room with wood paneling.

With Sensory Percussion, you can record studio-quality drum tracks anywhere (bedrooms and garages work great) with unlimted control of sounds in post production.

  Bringing the latest academic research to music creation.

Sensory Percussion's aim is to digitize the centuries-old mode of music creation – physical gestures, expressive movements, manipulation of physical objects – to change the way we make and perform music. We want to shift the music industry away from a mechanized and rigid recording and performance practice, back to an expressive and more human way of making music.
